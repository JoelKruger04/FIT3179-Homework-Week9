The Future of Public Transport in Melbourne

Present, past and future usage of public transport within greater Melbourne

Types of Public Transport

Melbourne has a range of public transport options, including trains, trams, buses, and coaches. Trains connect suburbs to the city, trams help people get around urban areas, and buses serve places not covered by the others. However, public transport usage is still lower than in 2019 because more people are working from home and travel habits have changed. This has led to talks about how to make the system better for today's commuters.
